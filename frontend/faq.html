Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Learning Management System

Are all courses available online?
Yes, all courses are available online. You can access them anytime after enrolling from any device with an internet connection.
Do I need prior experience to start?
No prior experience is required. Each course starts with basics and moves to advanced topics step by step, making it suitable for beginners.
Can I watch lectures multiple times?
Yes, you can watch all lectures as many times as you like once enrolled. There are no restrictions on rewatching content to reinforce your learning.
Will I get a certificate after completion?
Yes, upon successful completion of the course and passing all quizzes with a minimum score of 70%, a digital certificate will be provided that you can download and share.
How can I track my progress?
Your progress is automatically saved as you complete videos, readings, and quizzes. You can check it anytime from the course progress page where completed items are marked with blue circles.
How do quizzes work?
Quizzes contain multiple-choice questions to test your understanding. Select your answers and click submit to see your score immediately. A score of 70% or higher is considered passing.
Can I access courses from mobile devices?
Yes! Our platform is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers. Your progress syncs across all devices.
What happens when I complete a week?
When you complete all materials in a week (video, reading, and quiz), you'll receive a congratulations message and the week will be marked as complete with a blue circle in the sidebar.
How do I navigate between lectures?
Use the Previous/Next buttons at the bottom of each lecture, or click directly on any lecture in the course outline sidebar to jump to it immediately.
How can I get help if I have issues?
If you encounter any issues, check this FAQ page first. For additional support, contact our support team at support@scaler.com. We typically respond within 24 hours on business days.
